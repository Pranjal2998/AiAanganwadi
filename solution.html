Solution.

AI Anganwadi is a COMPLETE ECOSYSTEM designed for Anganwadis, aimed at empowering students, authorities, and Anganwadi centers. It provides monitoring capabilities, enhances teaching effectiveness, and facilitates digital learning for students.

Smartstart Board.

  • Quality Visual Content

    High quality graphics enhance understanding and encourage interactive engagement.

  • Interactive Learning Content

    Daily educational activities, aligned with NCERT curriculum, encourage hands on exploration.

  • 365 Day Curriculum

    Detailed daily plan ensures structured learning, improving education quality.

  • Anganwadi Worker’s Guide

    Comprehensive instructions for Anganwadi workers, aiding in teaching methodologies and quick problem solving.

  • High Speed Internet

    Broadens awareness and perspectives, expanding horizons for both students and teachers.

Anganwadi Connect Application.

For Impact Partners:

Impact partners are dedicated individuals from respected NGOs or companies who help Anganwadis evaluate and empower students.

  • Conduct age wise educational and health assessments to evaluate student growth.
  • Connect with Anganwadi to make improvements in current status of children’s nutrition and education progress.
  • Connect virtually with Anganwadi students for educational activities.
  • Log details of physical visits, infrastructural inspection and their reports.
For Anganwadi workers:

The Anganwadi Connect App helps Anganwadi workers do their tasks easily and talk to district leaders. It makes reporting and messaging simple, ensuring things run smoothly.

  • Anganwadi workers can report daily activities and evaluations via the app.
  • The app provides detailed instructions and teaching methods for Anganwadi workers.
  • Workers can connect district authorities each other for quick problem solving.
  • Anganwadi workers can access the Training Materials inside the application.

Monitoring System.

Monitoring System can be used to monitor the overall progress of Anganwadis and the students. Any government authorities ensure smooth operation of Anganwadis and progress of learning outcomes of students.

  • District Officials can directly access and review the Anganwadis with the help of graphical data.
  • Keeps a log of the daily activities and track when each Anganwadi center opens and closes every day.
  • Can be used to view student’s Assessment Progress Report of Health and Learning at Anganwadis.
  • Officials can access the Infrastructural Gap reports generated by Impact Partners after the inspection.
  • Based on the data collected, authorities can identify problems, plan improvements, and close any gaps in services.
