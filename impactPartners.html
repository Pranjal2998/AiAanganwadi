Join as an Impact Partner.

“Step into a transformative journey with us and become an integral part of a groundbreaking mission to revolutionize early childhood education in India.”

Role of Impact Partner.

Pick an Anganwadi center based on geographical location, previous reports, and specific criteria.

Collect detailed data on student health, nutrition education, Anganwadi workers' effectiveness, infrastructure conditions, and learning progress.

Compile findings, include your inputs and recommendations for improvements, and submit comprehensive reports for further evaluation and decision-making.

You are eligible to apply for the Impact Partner if you...

Individual Criteria
  • Individuals having a strong passion for education and community development.
  • Relevant experience in education, technology, or social sectors is beneficial.
  • any school going, working professional, etc willing to actively engage, contribute insights, and support initiatives aimed at improving early childhood education outcomes.
Criteria for Organizations:
  • If your organization is already associated with us, you are eligible to partner if you demonstrate a strong commitment to education, social impact, and innovation.
  • Alignment with CSR goals related to education and community development is essential.
  • Prior experience in educational initiatives or technology adoption in social sectors is advantageous.

Selection Process.

  • Fill Out the Form

    Complete a form gathering your contact details and organization information. This step helps us understand your background and how you can contribute effectively.

  • Understand the Initiative

    After receiving your form, we will connect with you to provide detailed information about our initiatives, goals, and how you can play a pivotal role as an impact partner.

  • Connect with Anganwadi

    Optionally, consider making a virtual or physical visit to an Anganwadi center. This visit allows you to gain firsthand insights into the current educational environment and challenges faced by Anganwadi workers and children. Following the visit, provide us with a comprehensive report outlining observations, recommendations, and potential areas of support.

  • Become a Impact Innovator

    If the visit reinforces your commitment and alignment with our mission, consider formalizing your role as a permanent impact partner. This commitment ensures sustained support and involvement in our efforts to transform early childhood education across India.
