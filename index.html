Problem Statement

In India, 45 million children begin their education at 1.3 million Anganwadi centers, which are essential for their educational foundation. However, these centers often fail to provide a strong start, leading children to attend mainly for basic needs rather than learning experiences.

  1. 1

    Outdated Teaching Methods:

    Basic, outdated curriculum results in poor learning outcomes and missed essential skills.

  2. 2

    Lack of Monitoring:

    Minimal supervision leads to ineffective operations and unverified benefits for children.

  3. 3

    Inadequate Worker Training:

    Workers lack proper training, hindering their ability to teach and care effectively.

  4. 4

    Uncoordinated Improvement Efforts:

    Disorganized efforts waste resources and impede progress, depriving children of intended benefits.

Process to Transform Anganwadis

AI Anganwadi is an AI-based smart board solution designed to enhance education at Anganwadi centers by providing monitoring capabilities, improving teaching effectiveness, and facilitating digital learning.

Smartstart Board

Features high-quality visual content, interactive learning aligned with NCERT curriculum, a 365-day structured plan, a comprehensive worker's guide, and high-speed internet.

Anganwadi Connect Application

Enables impact partners to conduct education & health assessments, improve children's nutrition and education, and log visit details. It helps workers with reporting, connecting with authorities, and accessing training materials.

Monitoring System

Allows district officials to monitor Anganwadis, track daily activities, review student progress, access infrastructural gap reports, and plan improvements based on collected data.
